Why It Exists

The tools were never built for us.

Most screening tools were designed using data from men, tested on men, and interpreted through a male lens. When women show up with the same conditions, they look different — quieter, more social, better hidden. So they get missed.

The average woman waits 12 years longer than a man for an ADHD diagnosis. Autistic women are routinely told they're "just anxious." Executive dysfunction gets labelled as laziness or lack of ambition.

NeuroBloom exists because the gap isn't in women — it's in the tools.

We built this around real female experiences: the exhaustion of performing normalcy, the mental load of over-preparing for conversations, the shame spiral after a missed deadline. Not symptoms. Patterns. And patterns can be understood.

You were never broken. You were just using someone else's map.
