Core Journey

Discover, Understand, and Thrive

Every feature maps to one of three phases. We take you from confusion and burnout to clarity and proactive support, creating a safe space along the way.

01

Discover

The "Finally, someone gets me" moment. Grounded in women's lived experiences, not clinical checklists.

🎯

Adaptive Screening

A dynamic questionnaire that adapts in real time. We don't ask "Do you have trouble focusing?" We ask: "Do you find yourself doing everything perfectly on the outside but feeling completely exhausted inside?"

02

Understand

Not a diagnosis, a mirror. See your behavioral and emotional patterns reflected back to you.

🎭

Masking Score

Measure the hidden cost of appearing "normal." See how much energy you spend masking, where you mask the most, and your risk of burnout.

🗺️

Personal Trait Map

A visual map of your focus tendencies, emotional sensitivity, sensory responses, and executive function profile. Non-diagnostic and empowering.

💡

AI Insight Engine

The "how did it know that?" moment. Receive a personalized, human-feeling narrative about why you struggle, written in warm, deeply affirming language.

03

Thrive

Practical, immediate tools and safety nets tailored to your brain.

🌿

Coping Strategy Matcher

Not a generic list of tips, but 5-6 highly actionable strategies that directly match your personal trait map—giving you immediate clarity.

⏳

Focus Recovery

"I lost the plot." A non-judgmental guided flow to help you find the single next tiny action, supported by a gentle body-doubling timer.

🌧️

Calm Down Mode

"I'm overwhelmed." The entire app simplifies to reduce cognitive load. A 30-second tactile grounding exercise gives you immediate sensory relief.

💬

Communication Coach

A private sandbox to reframe messages, stop over-explaining, and confidently set boundaries. Prep mode gets you ready for difficult conversations.

📚

Learning Mode

Can't read that wall of text? Transform any document into prioritized bullets, paced learning, real-world analogies, or listen with voice mode.

🔋

Social Recharge Tracker

Log energy drops in 10 seconds. The app learns your burnout cues and proactively nudges you to protect your peace before overstimulation hits.
